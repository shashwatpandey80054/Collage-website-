About Us

“Established in 2016, Government Polytechnic Chopan, Sonbhadra has rapidly grown into one of the most respected polytechnic institutes in Uttar Pradesh. The institute operates under the Directorate of Technical Education, Lucknow, aiming to equip young students with modern technical skills and enable them to make meaningful contributions to India’s development.”

Our Diploma Programs

💻 计算机科学与工程

Focus on programming, software development and networking. Prepare students for careers in IT companies, start-ups and digital innovation.。

⚡电子工程

Covers circuits, communication systems, and embedded technologies. Ideal for careers in telecommunications, automation, and electronics.

🚗 汽车工程

Focusing on automotive design, engine and electric vehicle technologies, we train future engineers for India's rapidly growing automotive industry.

超越学术

We promote inclusive, skills-based learning and industry experience through workshops, seminars and visits. In partnership with Skill India, we help students become not only job-ready but also entrepreneurial and innovative.
