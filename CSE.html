Computer Science and Engineering

The Diploma in Computer Science and Engineering is a 3-year full-time program covering computation, automation, data science, algorithms, database systems, theory of computation, and software design. Eligible candidates must have passed 10th or higher. Admission is based on the JEECUP entrance exam.
